About

What is Episteme?

Episteme is a growing collection of physics models and simulations designed to make complex physical concepts easier to understand and explore. Each simulation is paired with clear documentation — from how the math works, to how the code was written — so that anyone can follow the logic behind the physics. Whether it's a classical pendulum or quantum mechanics, the goal is to make each topic approachable, visual, and intuitive.

Vision

Many students around the world lack access to experimental setups or quality resources to study physics deeply. Episteme exists to fill that gap — by creating open, hands-on models of the very experiments and ideas that shape modern physics. The platform aims to inspire curiosity and support learning through visual, interactive, and well-explained tools. It's not just about watching a simulation — it's about understanding why it works.
