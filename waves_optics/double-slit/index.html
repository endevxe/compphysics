Double Slit Interference Simulation

This simulation models the intensity pattern produced by Young’s double-slit experiment. The formula is based on the small‑angle approximation:

\( I(x) \propto \cos^2\left( \frac{\pi d x}{\lambda L} \right) \)

where:

  • \( d \) is slit separation
  • \( \lambda \) is wavelength
  • \( L \) is distance to the screen
The result is a series of bright and dark fringes representing constructive and destructive interference.
